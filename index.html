To start, click on a tile Tools Info
  • Perlin search

    A circle moves on the screen, leaving its trace. Its movement is defined by perlin noise. Upon moving your mouse or finger along the x-axis, the radius of the circle changes.
  • Perlin Circle

    A circle moves on the y-axis according to where the mouse or finger is and leaves a trace. The movement on the x-axis is defined by perlin noise.
  • Moving square

    A square bouncing across the background, leaving its trace. Upon moving your mouse or finger across the screen, you'll find that the appearance of the square changes.
  • Arcs

    Repeated circle halves are moving on the screen according to the movement of the mouse or finger in a very chaotic manner.
  • Half a circle

    Repeated circle halves are moving on the screen according to the movement of the mouse or finger and leaving their trace. The other half of the circle is toggled by a click or touch.
  • Zooming and moving

    A rectangle of circles, which is able to be moved across the screen in multiple visually interesting ways. It's able to be zoomed in, rotated or dragged.
  • Particles

    Upon drawing a line, it will slowly fall and bounce of the walls. According to where it's drawn it will fall slower or faster and the circles, the line is made of are bigger or smaller.
  • Check pattern

    An interactive check pattern sort of, which leaves a trace. Upon moving your mouse or finger across the screen, you'll find the circles move.
  • Feathers or needles

    A horizontal formation morphing between appearing like a bunch of feathers and needles.
  • Squaremania

    A square moving in a scanner-like formation across the background. Upon moving your mouse or finger across the screen, you'll find that the appearance of the square changes.
  • Moving circle

    A circle bouncing across the background, leaving its trace. Upon moving your mouse or finger across the screen, you'll find that the appearance of the circle changes.
